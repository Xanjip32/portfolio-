That hands-on problem-solving experience now shapes how I approach code.

Hindi

Devanagari Script

Fluent Linguistic Cousin of Nepali

Hindi is a linguistic cousin of Nepali — both descend from Sanskrit and share the Devanagari writing system. I grew up understanding Hindi through media, culture, and regional proximity. It's mutually intelligible with Nepali in many everyday situations.

About Hindi

  • 600+ million speakers worldwide
  • Official language of India
  • Shares Devanagari script and Sanskrit roots with Nepali
  • Often mutually intelligible with Nepali
