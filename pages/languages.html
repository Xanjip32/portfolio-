Languages

Communication across cultures is a skill I value. Growing up multilingual and living abroad has shaped how I connect with people.

🇳🇵

Nepali

Devanagari Script

Native Mother Tongue

Nepali is my mother tongue and the language I grew up speaking at home. It's the official language of Nepal and is spoken by over 25 million people worldwide. I use it daily with family and friends from home.

About Nepali

  • 25+ million speakers worldwide
  • Official language of Nepal
  • Uses Devanagari script (same as Hindi, Sanskrit)
  • Part of the Indo-Aryan language family
🇮🇳

Hindi

Devanagari Script

Fluent Linguistic Cousin of Nepali

Hindi is a linguistic cousin of Nepali — both descend from Sanskrit and share the Devanagari writing system. I grew up understanding Hindi through media, culture, and regional proximity. It's mutually intelligible with Nepali in many everyday situations.

About Hindi

  • 600+ million speakers worldwide
  • Official language of India
  • Shares Devanagari script and Sanskrit roots with Nepali
  • Often mutually intelligible with Nepali
🇬🇧

English

Latin Script

Intermediate–Upper IELTS 6.5

English is the language I use for academics, coding, and professional work. I scored 6.5 on IELTS, which reflects my ability to communicate effectively in academic and workplace settings. I'm comfortable with technical documentation, presentations, and daily conversation.

About English

  • IELTS Academic: 6.5
  • Used daily for coding and academics
  • Global lingua franca for tech and business
  • Primary language of instruction at Youngsan University
🇰🇷

Korean

Hangul Script

Basic–Conversational TOPIK II (Level 2)

I'm currently studying Korean while living in Busan, South Korea. I've achieved TOPIK Level 2, which covers basic to intermediate proficiency. I can handle daily conversations, read Hangul, and navigate everyday life. I continue to improve through immersion and coursework.

About Korean

  • TOPIK II — Level 2 (Basic–Intermediate)
  • Studying at Youngsan University in Busan
  • Daily life conversations and navigation
  • Can read Hangul and basic texts
